About Us

5th largest State
Economy In Nigeria

Oyo State, a thriving economic hub in southwestern Nigeria, offers compelling opportunities for investors across various sectors. The state's government has made significant strides to strengthen its economy, making it one of the most promising locations for investment in Nigeria

Oyo State’s GDP ranks among the highest in Nigeria.

Excellent Interconnectivity

Oyo State offers robust connectivity with efficient road networks, expanding rail links, and proximity to major air and seaports.

02

Prime Workforce

Oyo State is home to a skilled and youthful workforce, supported by a strong educational network that includes renowned universities, technical institutes, and vocational centers.

03

Large Landmass

With one of the largest land areas in Nigeria, Oyo State offers vast opportunities for agriculture, industrial expansion, and real estate development.

04

Growing Economy

Oyo State’s economy is experiencing dynamic growth, driven by strategic investments in agriculture, manufacturing, and technology, supported by a business-friendly environment and government-backed incentives.
