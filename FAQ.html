FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the FAQ page of the Oyo State Investment Promotion Agency. Here, we provide answers to the most common questions about investing in Oyo State, our agency’s services, and more. If you have additional questions, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

General Information

What is the role of the Oyo State Investment Promotion Agency (OYSIPA)?

OYSIPA is dedicated to promoting and facilitating investments in Oyo State. We assist potential investors by providing information on investment opportunities, simplifying regulatory processes, and offering support throughout the investment process to ensure smooth operations for new and existing businesses.

What sectors are open to investment in Oyo State?

Oyo State offers investment opportunities across a range of sectors, including Agribusiness, Real Estate, Infrastructure, Renewable Energy, Education, Technology, Healthcare, Tourism, Mining, and more. Our agency can provide sector-specific information to help guide your investment decisions.

Investment Process

How can I register my business in Oyo State?

To register a business, you’ll need to follow the guidelines provided by the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria. OYSIPA can assist in connecting you to the right resources and provide support during the registration process. Learn more about business registration here.

What incentives are available for investors?

Oyo State offers various incentives to encourage investment, including tax incentives, land acquisition support, and access to funding opportunities. Specific incentives may vary by sector. Contact us to learn more about the incentives available for your chosen industry.

How long does it take to set up a business in Oyo State?

The time required to set up a business depends on the business type and regulatory requirements. However, our agency is committed to making the process as quick and smooth as possible by assisting with the necessary approvals and permits.

Support Services

Does OYSIPA offer support after business establishment?

Yes, OYSIPA provides ongoing support to investors even after their businesses are established. This includes assistance with regulatory compliance, networking opportunities, and continuous guidance on growth and expansion within Oyo State.

How can I get more information on specific investment projects?

For detailed information on specific investment projects, please contact our Investment Promotion Office. Our team will be happy to provide you with project details, feasibility studies, and other relevant data to support your investment decisions.

Contact and Inquiry

How can I reach the Investment Promotion Office?

You can reach our office by phone, email, or in person. Visit our Contact Us page for detailed information on how to get in touch with our team.

Is there an online form for submitting investment inquiries?

Yes, we offer an Investment Inquiry Form on our website. This form allows potential investors to submit specific questions or requests, and our team will respond promptly to provide assistance.

If you have further questions that are not covered in this FAQ, please feel free to reach out to us directly. Our team is here to help make your investment journey in Oyo State as smooth and rewarding as possible.
