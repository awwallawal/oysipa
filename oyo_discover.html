Discover Oyo State

Key Information About Oyo State

Why Invest in Oyo State?

Oyo State is strategically located in southwestern Nigeria, with proximity to Lagos and major transportation routes. With an area of over 28,000 sq. km and a population of 8.3 million, the state boasts a dynamic economy and serves as a major trade hub.

Advantages for Investors

  • Proximity to Lagos Port: Oyo State’s capital, Ibadan, is a quick train ride from the Lagos Port, facilitating efficient cargo transport.
  • Growing Youth Workforce: Around 70% of Oyo’s population falls within the productive age range, ensuring a steady supply of skilled labor.
  • Free Trade Zones: The Oluyole Free Trade Zone and Pacesetter Industrial Park offer tax advantages and incentives for export-oriented businesses.
  • Tax Structure: The state has streamlined its tax structure to eliminate double taxation, providing a more business-friendly environment.

Key Sectors for Investment

Oyo State offers a range of opportunities across various sectors:

  • Agribusiness: With extensive arable land, Oyo State is ideal for agricultural production and processing industries.
  • Renewable Energy: There is growing potential for solar and other renewable energy investments to serve local industries and communities.
  • Solid Minerals: Rich mineral deposits offer investment opportunities in mining and related processing industries.
  • Technology and Education: The state encourages investment in educational facilities and tech-driven initiatives, reflecting its strong focus on skills development.

Infrastructure and Accessibility

The state government is committed to infrastructure improvement. Key initiatives include:

  • Ongoing upgrades to the Ibadan Inland Dry Port for seamless cargo handling.
  • An efficient road network that reduces traffic and ensures smooth intra-state travel.
  • Quick processing times for permits, with Certificates of Occupancy typically issued within three months.

Business-Friendly Policies

Oyo State promotes a secure environment through investments in safety and law enforcement, including the Western Nigeria Security Network. Business permits and registrations are processed efficiently to support investors’ needs. The state government’s proactive approach ensures streamlined processes for land acquisition and tax registration.

Get Started

Oyo State offers a welcoming environment for both local and international investors, with resources like the Oyo State Public-Private Partnership Agency (OYSIPA) acting as a one-stop shop for investment inquiries and support. To begin investing in Oyo State, contact OYSIPA at info@oysipa.oy.gov.ng or call +2348090890005.
